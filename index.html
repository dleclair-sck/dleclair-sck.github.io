Tax Strategy

Cost Segregation: Keep More of What You Earn

Owning a short-term rental property isn't just about monthly income — it's one of the most powerful tax strategy vehicles available to investors today. SoCal Keys connects our clients with cost segregation specialists who help you dramatically reduce your tax burden through accelerated depreciation.

⚡
Accelerated Depreciation
Cost segregation breaks your property into components that depreciate faster — allowing you to front-load deductions and dramatically reduce taxable income in the early years of ownership.
🏦
Significant Tax Savings
Many STR property owners save tens of thousands of dollars in taxes in their first year alone through a properly executed cost segregation study — money that goes directly back into your pocket or next investment.
🔗
Expert Referral Network
We connect our property owners with trusted cost segregation specialists and STR-savvy CPAs who understand the specific tax advantages available to short-term rental investors.
📊
STR-Specific Tax Advantages
Short-term rental properties qualify for unique tax treatments not available to traditional rentals. We ensure our clients know every advantage available to them as STR owners.
Turn Your Property Into a Tax Strategy — Not Just an Income Stream

Most STR owners leave significant money on the table at tax time. A cost segregation study — combined with the right tax professional — can transform your property from a simple income source into a sophisticated wealth-building vehicle.
